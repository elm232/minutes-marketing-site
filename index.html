Minutes

Your content, on your schedule.

Features

  • Take Control of Your Reading List

    Cut down that Pocket queue you've been building up, since all your favorite bloggers have way more free time than you do.

  • Only As Much Content As You Have Time For

    Take a couple minutes, or take half an hour. Minute selects content that fits into YOUR schedule.

  • A Beautiful Platform for Reading, Available on the Go

    Tune the reading interface to match your preferred reading style, and take it with you on your phone or tablet.

Get The App

Go to your settings app, then go to the security section. Under the "Device administration" section, enable the option for "Unknown sources".

Download the apk, browse to it using your phone's file manager, and try to open it.

It should open with the package installer, where you can install choose to install it just like any other app.
